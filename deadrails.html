Dead Rails

Four players. One train. An endless tide of the dead between here and the next stop.

Year2025
RoleSolo Environment Artist
EngineUnreal Engine 5
Players1–4 Co-op
The Vision

Keep moving
or die trying

✦

Dead Rails began with a single design question: what if the objective was also the timer? In a post-apocalyptic world stripped down to one lifeline — the railroad — the train isn't just transport. It's the anchor, the fuel gauge, and the thing worth dying for.

Every environment was built around that tension. Survivor camps designed to tempt players off the train, open terrain that rewards aggression and punishes hesitation, and train shelters that funnel the horde into brutal chokepoints. The world exists to pressure the team, not just fill space.

Built in Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen global illumination, the lighting shifts from the cold blue desolation of open track to the desperate warmth of survivor fires — each zone communicating danger and safety without a single word of UI.
