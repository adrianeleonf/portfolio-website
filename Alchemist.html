Alchemist Study

A cathedral of forbidden knowledge — where dust, candlelight, and centuries of obsession converge.

Year2024
RoleSolo Environment Artist
EngineUnreal Engine 5
Duration6 Weeks
The Vision

Between knowledge
and obsession

✦

The Alchemist Study began as a question: what does a lifetime of forbidden pursuit look like as a space? The answer emerged as a towering interior draped in parchment, candlelight, and the residue of a hundred failed transmutations.

Every surface tells a story — shelves bowing under the weight of esoteric texts, a desk scarred by acid burns, candles melted to stubs beside half-translated manuscripts. The environment was designed not just to be seen but to be felt, to carry the weight of obsessive, decades-long solitude.

Built in Unreal Engine 5 with Lumen global illumination, the lighting was the central creative challenge. Achieving the sensation of a room where candlelight is simultaneously the only warmth and a source of creeping dread required careful layering of emissive surfaces, point lights, and atmospheric fog volumes.
