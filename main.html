The Artist

Between shadow
and light

✦

Adrian Leon is a Chicago-based 3D Environment Artist originally from Guatemala, specializing in creating surreal, immersive worlds for games. His portfolio features projects ranging from expansive, detailed landscapes to atmospheric interior environments, crafted using tools such as Blender, Unreal Engine, and Substance Painter. Adrian blends his technical expertise with a passion for storytelling to create visually captivating, narrative-driven game spaces.

In addition to designing 3D environments, Adrian has a deep love for writing about the worlds he creates. He develops lore and narratives that give depth and context to his environments, ensuring each world is not only visually striking but also rich with meaning and emotional resonance. This storytelling is central to his creative process, bringing life and purpose to the spaces he designs.

Adrian is actively seeking opportunities in the gaming industry to further refine his craft, collaborate with other creatives, and push the boundaries of immersive world design. Outside of his work, he finds inspiration in music. Specifically post-rock and progressive metal He enjoys discovering new bands, attending live shows, and playing guitar. Surrounded by art in all its forms, Adrian draws on a wide range of influences to fuel his creative vision.
